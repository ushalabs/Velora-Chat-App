# Account Deletion – Velora

Users can request deletion of their Velora account and associated data by contacting us via email.

## How to request deletion:

1. Send an email to: [ushakurosaki@gmail.com](mailto:ushakurosaki@gmail.com)
2. Use subject: "Account Deletion Request"
3. Include your registered email address

## What will be deleted:

* Account information
* Messages and media (where applicable)
* Profile data

Requests will be processed within 5–7 business days.
